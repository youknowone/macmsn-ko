Save and view a conversation history

Show All

You can save and view a text conversation in an area of Messenger called the Conversation History. In the Conversation History, text conversations are organized by contact name and then by date. All conversations that you save with a contact on a particular date are saved in a single log.

When you save a conversation in Conversation History, only the text portion is saved. Audio and video are not saved. If you use the Project Center in Entourage, you can also save a single conversation as a note in a project.

Do any of the following:

Set preferences for saving all conversations

Messenger can save conversations automatically, never save conversations, or ask whether to save each conversation. You must set your preferences separately for each account.

Important

Before you set preferences for saving, you must complete at least one conversation with that account. Otherwise, Messenger does not save your preferences.

  1. Sign in to your personal accountAn account on the Windows Live network, including all e-mail addresses ending in "@live.com", "@msn.com", and "@hotmail.com". With a personal account you can communicate with other contacts on the Windows Live and Yahoo networks. or corporate accountAn account on a corporate network that uses the Office Communications Server 2007. With a corporate account, you can communicate with other people in your network and in other federated networks. You can also use a corporate account to communicate with contacts on the Windows Live, MSN, MSN Hotmail, Yahoo, and AOL networks..

  2. On the Messenger menu, click Preferences, and then click History .

  3. Depending on the account that you want to set preferences for, click the Personal or Corporate tab.

  4. Select the options that you want.

    To limit how long conversations are saved, click an option on the Delete all conversations pop-up menu.

Save a single conversation

If you turn Save conversations off, you can still save a single conversation in the Conversation History and as a note in an Entourage project.

  1. In the conversation window, click Save .

  2. Select the options that you want.

    The conversation is saved when you close the conversation window.

View a saved conversation

  1. Sign in to the account for which you want to view the history.

  2. On the View menu, click Conversation History.

  3. In the navigation pane, click All Archived Personal Messages or All Archived Corporate Messages, and then click a contact.

  4. On the right side, click the date of the conversation.

Tips 

  • To search through the Conversation History, click the folder in the left pane that you want to search in. Type a word or phrase in the Search box, and then press ENTER. Conversations that contain the search term appear in the upper-right pane.

  • To delete a conversation from the Conversation History, select the conversation, and then click Delete .
