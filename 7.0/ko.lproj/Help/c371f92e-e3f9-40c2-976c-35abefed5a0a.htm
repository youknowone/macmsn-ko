Clear a password or user accounts

Show All

To improve security, you can clear passwords and user accounts that are stored in Messenger. Clearing passwords and user accounts is especially important if multiple people use the same computer.

Do any of the following:

Clear a password that is stored in Messenger
Important

If you have multiple accounts set up in Messenger, you must clear each password separately.

  1. On the View menu, make sure that there is a check mark next to Personal Contact List or Corporate Contact List for the account whose password you want to clear.

  2. Close any conversation windows.

  3. If you are already signed in to Messenger, on the Network menu, click Sign Out.

  4. In the contacts window, click Sign in with a different account or change your online status.

  5. On the E-mail address pop-up menu, click the account whose password you want to clear.

  6. Clear the Remember my password check box.

Note When you clear a password from Messenger, the password is not deleted from the keychain on your computer. For information about the keychain, see Mac Help.

Clear all user accounts that are stored in Messenger

You should clear user accounts if you used Messenger on a public computer. You might also want to clear user accounts if you allowed someone to sign in to his or her Messenger account on your computer.

When you clear user accounts, account IDs, passwords, display picturesThe image next to your name in the Personal Contacts window. You can change your display picture for personal accounts, but not for corporate accounts.

, and display names are deleted. Contacts are not deleted from accounts.

Note To clear personal and corporate accounts, you must follow these steps in both the Personal Contacts and the Corporate Contacts windows.

  1. On the View menu, make sure that there is a check mark next to Personal Contact List or Corporate Contact List for the kind of accounts that you want to clear.

  2. Close any conversation windows.

  3. If you are already signed in to Messenger, on the Network menu, click Sign Out.

  4. In the contacts window, click Sign in with a different account or change your online status.

  5. Click Clear Account History, and then click Clear History.

Important

When you clear user accounts, saved conversations are not deleted from the Conversation History. To delete conversations from the Conversation History, on the View menu, click Conversation History. Select the conversations that you want to delete, and then click Delete .
