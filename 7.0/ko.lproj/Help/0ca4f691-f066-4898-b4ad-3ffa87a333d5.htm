Add an emoticon to a message

Show All

Emoticons are graphic images that you can use to express your emotions within a message.

  1. Click in the conversation window.

  2. Click , and then click the emoticon that you want.

Notes 

  • To turn off emoticons, on the Messenger menu, click Preferences, and then click Appearance. Under Message Font Style, clear the check boxes for the emoticons that you don't want.

  • With a personal accountAn account on the Windows Live network, including all e-mail addresses ending in "@live.com", "@msn.com", and "@hotmail.com". With a personal account you can communicate with other contacts on the Windows Live and Yahoo networks., you can add additional emoticons to the emoticon menu. Click , and then click . To add an emoticon, select its check box.

  • Depending on a contact's messaging application and messaging service, custom emoticons and some standard emoticons might not be visible to that contact.
