See who added you to his or her contact list

[This topic is pre-release documentation and is subject to change in future releases. Blank topics are included as placeholders.]

  1. Sign in to the account that you want to check.

  2. On the Messenger menu, click Preferences, and then click Privacy .

  3. Depending on the kind of account that you want to check, click the Personal or Corporate tab.

  4. Next to Which users have added me to their contact list, click View.
